DATA PRIVACY POLICY

Fanbase Collective Incorporated (“Fanbase Collective,” “FBC,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects the privacy of our clients and their customers.

This Data Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, where it comes from, how we use it, how we process it, how we share it, how we protect it, how long we retain it, how unsubscribe requests are handled, and the choices available regarding that information.

This Data Privacy Policy applies to FanbaseCollective.com, FBaseCollective.com, our software, reports, audience tools, recommendation tools, campaign tools, integrations, communications, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

By accessing or using the Services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Data Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Fanbase Collective collects information directly from clients and from connected platforms authorized by clients.

Client Information

We may collect:

  • Business name
  • Business email address
  • Subscription information
  • Billing information

Customer Information

When authorized by a client through a connected platform, we may receive:

  • Customer name
  • Customer email address
  • Customer identifier

Purchase Information

When authorized through a connected platform such as Square, we may receive:

  • Transaction date
  • Transaction amount
  • Products purchased
  • Purchase history
  • Visit history

Product Information

We may receive:

  • Product names
  • Product categories
  • Product pricing
  • Product sales activity

2. Where Information Comes From

Information may be collected from:

  • Clients
  • Connected platforms authorized by clients
  • Subscription records
  • Billing records

Connected platforms may include:

  • Square
  • Other platforms authorized by the client

3. How We Use Information

Fanbase Collective uses information to:

  • Create customer audiences
  • Generate customer reports
  • Generate product recommendations
  • Build email campaigns
  • Send email campaigns
  • Measure campaign performance
  • Process unsubscribe requests
  • Maintain suppression lists
  • Operate and support the Services

Examples of reports and audiences may include:

  • Customers Slowing Down
  • Lost Customers
  • Revenue At Risk
  • Customer Spending Reports
  • Customer Visit Reports
  • Customer Retention Reports

4. How We Process Information

Fanbase Collective processes information solely to provide the Services requested by the client.

Processing activities may include:

  • Importing customer information
  • Importing purchase information
  • Importing product information
  • Organizing customer information
  • Creating customer audiences
  • Generating reports
  • Generating recommendations
  • Building campaigns
  • Sending email campaigns
  • Measuring campaign performance
  • Processing unsubscribe requests
  • Maintaining suppression lists

🛡️ Fanbase Collective does not sell, rent, or use customer information for unrelated advertising purposes.

5. Client Data Ownership

Clients retain ownership of their Client Data.

Client Data includes information relating to:

  • Customers
  • Purchases
  • Products
  • Campaigns
  • Audiences

Fanbase Collective does not acquire ownership rights to Client Data. Client Data is processed solely to provide the Services requested by the client.

6. Email Communications and Unsubscribe Requests

Fanbase Collective may assist clients in creating and sending email campaigns through the Services.

When a recipient chooses to unsubscribe from communications sent through the Services:

  • The unsubscribe request may be processed through the platform.
  • The recipient may be added to a suppression list.
  • Future campaigns may exclude suppressed recipients.
  • Fanbase Collective may maintain suppression records to help prevent future communications to unsubscribed recipients.

Clients remain responsible for obtaining permissions necessary to communicate with their customers and for complying with applicable marketing laws.

Fanbase Collective does not sell Client Data. Fanbase Collective does not rent Client Data. Information may be shared only as necessary to operate the Services.

Examples include:

  • Security providers
