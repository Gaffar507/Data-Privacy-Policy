DATA PRIVACY POLICY

Fanbase Collective Incorporated (“Fanbase Collective,” “FBC,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects the privacy of our customers and their customers.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we process it, how we protect it, and the choices available regarding that information.

This Data Privacy Policy applies to FanbaseCollective.com, FBaseCollective.com, our software, reports, customer audience tools, recommendation tools, campaign tools, integrations, communications, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

Fanbase Collective collects information directly from businesses using our Services and from connected systems authorized by those businesses.

Business Information

We may collect:

  • Business name
  • Business address
  • Business phone number
  • Business email address
  • Website address
  • Subscription information
  • Account credentials
  • Billing information

Customer Information

When authorized by a business, we may receive:

  • Customer names
  • Customer email addresses
  • Customer identifiers
  • Customer account information

Transaction Information

When authorized through connected platforms such as Square, we may receive:

  • Order information
  • Transaction dates
  • Purchase history
  • Product purchases
  • Transaction amounts
  • Visit history
  • Location information
  • Customer spending activity

Product Information

We may collect:

  • Product names
  • Product categories
  • Product pricing
  • Product sales activity
  • Product performance information

Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Session information
  • Usage information
  • Website activity
  • Login activity

2. How We Use Information

We use information to provide the Services requested by our customers.

Examples include:

Customer Reporting

Information may be used to generate reports including:

  • Customers slowing down
  • Lost customers
  • Revenue at risk
  • Customer activity reports
  • Customer spending reports
  • Customer visit reports

Audience Creation

Information may be used to create customer audiences based on:

  • Purchase history
  • Spending activity
  • Visit frequency
  • Product purchases
  • Customer behavior

Recommendation Campaigns

Information may be used to generate:

  • Product recommendations
  • Frequently bought together suggestions
  • Customers also bought suggestions
  • Trending product suggestions

Campaign Creation

Information may be used to:

  • Build email campaigns
  • Select campaign recipients
  • Measure campaign performance
  • Track campaign engagement

Platform Operations

Information may be used to:

  • Maintain the Services
  • Provide support
  • Improve functionality
  • Detect fraud
  • Protect security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Process Information

Fanbase Collective processes information solely to provide the Services requested by the business using the platform.

Processing activities may include:

  • Importing customer records
  • Importing transaction history
  • Organizing customer information
  • Creating customer audiences
  • Generating reports
  • Generating recommendations
  • Sending email campaigns
  • Measuring campaign performance
  • Maintaining suppression records
  • Processing unsubscribe requests

🛡️ Fanbase Collective does not sell, rent, or use customer information for unrelated advertising purposes.

4. Customer Data Ownership

Businesses retain ownership of their customer data. Fanbase Collective does not acquire ownership rights to customer data. Customer data is processed solely for the purpose of providing the Services.

5. Email Communications and Unsubscribe Requests

Fanbase Collective may assist businesses in sending email campaigns through the Services.

When a recipient chooses to unsubscribe through an email campaign sent using the Services:

  • The unsubscribe request is processed through the platform.
  • The recipient may be added to a suppression list.
  • Future campaigns may exclude suppressed recipients.

Businesses remain responsible for obtaining any permissions required to communicate with recipients and for complying with applicable marketing laws.

6. Information Sharing

Fanbase Collective does not sell customer information. Fanbase Collective does not rent customer information. Information may be shared only as necessary to operate the Services.

Examples include:

  • Email delivery providers
  • Cloud infrastructure providers
  • Database providers
  • Security providers
  • Payment processors

7. Service Providers

Fanbase Collective relies upon trusted service providers to operate the Services. These providers may include:

  • Square
  • Cloudflare

  • Postmark

These providers may process information only as necessary to provide their services. Customers should review the privacy policies of connected third party providers.

8. Security

Fanbase Collective maintains commercially reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. Security measures may include:

  • Encrypted connections
  • Access controls

  • Infrastructure security controls
  • Monitoring and logging

No system can guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Services, maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect platform security.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information involved.

10. Data Deletion

Businesses may request deletion of account information by contacting Fanbase Collective.

Certain information may be retained where required by law, security obligations, fraud prevention requirements, contractual obligations, or legitimate business purposes.

11. Cookies and Analytics

Fanbase Collective may use cookies and similar technologies to maintain sessions, improve functionality, measure website usage, enhance security, and improve customer experience.

Users may manage cookie settings through their browser.

12. Children’s Privacy

The Services are intended for business use and are not directed toward children under thirteen (13) years of age. Fanbase Collective does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy
